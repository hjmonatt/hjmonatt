Michelle Monatt

Michelle is a dedicated licensed massage therapist offering 18 years of acute-care experience in Clinical Massage Therapy with solid credentials that include multi-state licensure in Connecticut, New York, California, New Mexico, and Washington. She is well versed in a broad range of various massage techniques resulting in structural and functional changes for the prevention/treatment of muscle injuries.

Michelle's goal is to educate her clients in self-care by empowering individuals to understand their body mechanics, posture, and the effects of one's lifestyle. In her 18 years of practice, Michelle has worked with personal trainers, instructors, cyclists, professional athletes, geriatric patients, chiropractic patients, physical therapy patients, bodybuilders, and anyone else who may be struggling with stress and muscle pain.
