Bio

Heather Joy Monatt

I hold a BA in Film and Media Studies from UC Irvine and have an extensive background in customer service with 10+ years in the retail, hospitality, and food service industries. I believe someone with my work experience is a valuable asset to any company, because I am able to quickly understand, anticipate, and deliver what my clients are looking for. It is fulfilling and brings me joy when I am able to serve others and help them attain whatever they may desire. My interests have been leaning more towards back-end programming, however my goal is to be a full-stack developer. Having my first degree in Film and Media Studies, I believe that it enables me to have a keen eye for aesthetic design. I would like to be able to take my previous work experience and education to the next level of my career path and combine it with what I have learned in programming to create projects that are helpful for users and satisfies them with whatever their needs may be.
